# Mission


## What is this?
A web-based tax calculator tailored for South African individual taxpayers, using current SARS (South African Revenue Service) tax tables and rules to estimate annual income tax, monthly PAYE, UIF contributions, and take-home pay. The goal is to make SARS tax calculations accessible and understandable without requiring users to read complex legislation.

## Who is it for?
South African individual taxpayers — salaried employees and freelancers — who want a quick, accurate estimate of their tax liability based on official SARS rates.

## Problem it solves
SARS tax tables and rebate rules are published but scattered across documents and the SARS website. Most people cannot easily calculate their own PAYE, apply the correct rebates, or understand how a salary change affects their take-home pay.

## Success looks like
- A user can enter a gross annual salary and immediately see their income tax, UIF, net pay, and effective tax rate.
- Tax brackets, rebates, and thresholds reflect the current SARS tax year (2024/2025).
- Results match SARS's own PAYE calculations to within a rounding margin.
- The app is usable on both desktop and mobile browsers without errors.

## Out of scope (v1)
- Company / corporate tax calculations
- Capital gains tax (CGT)
- Provisional tax and multiple income sources
- VAT calculations
- Integration with SARS eFiling
- User accounts or saved calculations
